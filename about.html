About me

Welcome to my personal portfolio. I am a dedicated and passionate web developer with a year of industry experience. Prior to my journey in tech, I worked extensively in the retail sector, which provided me with a unique perspective and skills that I've found to be invaluable in my current role.

In retail, I learned the importance of customer service, efficient problem-solving, and team collaboration. I've been able to apply these skills in web development, enhancing my approach to user experience, debugging, and team projects. It has also given me an in-depth understanding of customer behaviors and needs, allowing me to create more intuitive and user-friendly applications.

My web development interests lie in both front-end and back-end development, and I have a strong commitment to Test-Driven Development (TDD) to ensure the delivery of reliable and secure software.

When I'm not behind a computer screen crafting and debugging code, you can find me at the CrossFit gym. I am an active member of the community and believe that maintaining a healthy balance between work and personal life enhances productivity and creativity.
